# ABMS Brand Extraction Notes

Source: existing logo `ABMS-LOGO.png` from abmsconsulting.com.

## Extracted palette

- ABMS Red (primary brand): `#B02828` — the dominant accent in the logo, used heavily in the rectangular frame and the modular `abms` characters
- Deep Red (hover/active emphasis): `#8E1F1F` — a darker shade for hover, focus, and dense headlines on white
- Institutional Black: `#111111` — for the `CONSULTING` wordmark, body text, and dense editorial typography
- Charcoal (secondary text and rules): `#2F2E2E` — matches existing site SVG fill color
- Bone (off-white surface): `#F4F1EC` — paper-like background for editorial sections, references the citation aesthetic
- Mist (separator / muted): `#E5E1DA` — subtle horizontal rules and tonal section breaks
- Putrajaya Ink (dark hero plate): `#0E0E10` — for cinematic full-bleed hero photography overlays

## Type system

- Display / wordmarks: `Fraunces` (semi-bold to black) — sets institutional, archive-like authority. Used for hero declarations, section openers, and stat numerals.
- Body / running text: `Inter Tight` (regular to medium) — engineered legibility, matched x-height, used for paragraphs, labels, navigation, and metadata.
- Numerals & data: `JetBrains Mono` — only for ISO standard numbers (e.g., `ISO 37001:2016`), countdown digits, dates, and document references. The mono treatment performs the citation function.

## Design philosophy chosen

- Magazine-grade editorial layout with asymmetric column structure
- ABMS Red used sparingly as the single chromatic statement; otherwise the palette is institutional black, charcoal, and bone
- Hairline rules, small-caps eyebrows, and citation-style metadata replace generic SaaS section headings
- Photography is full-bleed Malaysian institutional infrastructure or operational scenes — never smiling teams or boardroom stock
- Animation is restrained: slow opacity reveals, tiny upward translations on scroll, no easing tricks
